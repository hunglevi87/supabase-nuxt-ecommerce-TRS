# Gift Concierge Starter Guide
Start in the Gift experience to build a box with curated vault items, then unlock rewards as your subtotal crosses tier thresholds.

## Suggested flow
1. Open Gift Builder and add your core items.
2. Review tier progress and unlock available rewards.
3. Browse testimonials and curated pre-made sets for fast add-ons.
4. Save your draft or proceed directly to checkout.
