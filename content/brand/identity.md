# The Relic Shop Brand Identity
**Tagline:** Rare, Elegant, Luxury Items Curated

## Core values
- Authenticity First
- Community Connection
- Curated Excellence
- Accessible Luxury

## Visual direction
- Bee crest as primary motif
- Golden snake frame as premium secondary motif
- Dark luxury palette with gold accents
- Serif-forward headline typography with modern UI body copy
